Welcome to the Duck Control Panel

Your centralized dashboard for overseeing all things quack-related. Whether you're monitoring pond activity, managing feeding schedules, or reviewing the latest waddling analytics, this is your command center for duck operations.

From hatchling setup to full-flight readiness, the Duck Control Panel gives you streamlined access to essential configuration parameters. Tailor your duck system’s startup behavior to suit your flock’s needs:

  • Startup Color – Choose the initial LED hue to reflect your preferred mood or flock identity. Whether it's classic mallard green or alert-mode amber, your ducks boot up in style.
  • Access Point Password – Secure your flock's wireless access with a configurable WPA2 passphrase. Keep unauthorized beavers and squirrels off your network.
  • Startup Duration – Define how long the pre-quack initialization sequence lasts before full pond readiness. Useful for sequencing coordinated waddles or synchronized splashes.
